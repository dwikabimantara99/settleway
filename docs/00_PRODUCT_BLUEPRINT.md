# 00 - Product Blueprint: Settleway

## Product identity

**Settleway** is a web marketplace for high-value agricultural commodity transactions. It connects farmers, suppliers, real stock-holding aggregators, distributors, large buyers, restaurants, factories, and food-business actors in a more open, safer, and more verifiable trade ecosystem.

Tagline: **The Safer Way to Settle Real-World Trade**

## Narrative foundation

Settleway is not only an escrow application. It is a full marketplace experience. Users can browse commodity listings, create buyer requests, view profiles, start a formal Deal Room, simulate deposits, submit evidence, settle transactions, and build reputation.

The difference between Settleway and an ordinary marketplace is the trust layer. Ordinary marketplaces can help buyer and seller discover each other, but the risk remains after discovery. Buyer fears the seller does not really hold the goods, quality is wrong, or delivery never happens. Seller fears the buyer does not pay, cancels unfairly, delays confirmation, or abuses disputes.

Settleway solves two large problems.

First, many small farmers and commodity actors have real goods, real land, real stock, or real harvest estimates, but do not have broad market access. They often depend on local collectors, middlemen, or closed networks.

Second, when a new buyer and seller meet, trust is still fragile. High-value commodity transactions cannot rely only on chat and informal promises. They need formal terms, escrow, commitment bonds, proof, and reputation.

## Core product formula

Settleway = Marketplace + Buyer Request + Pre-Harvest Listing + Deal Room + Simulated Bank Deposit + Stellar/Soroban Escrow Events + Dual Commitment Bonds + Proof Hash + Two-sided Reputation + Privacy-controlled Transaction Proof.

## Core demo story

A chili seller in Probolinggo lists 700 kg of red chili. A restaurant buyer in Surabaya opens the listing and creates a deal worth IDR 20,000,000. The buyer simulates a bank deposit for principal, buyer bond, and service fee. The seller deposits a performance bond and service fee. The Deal Room moves to locked state. The seller uploads evidence. Settleway hashes the evidence and records the hash/event on Stellar Testnet. The buyer accepts delivery. Settlement is recorded, payment is released, and both reputations update.

## Principles

- Marketplace discovery comes first; blockchain proves settlement behind the scenes.
- Deal Room is the formal transaction center.
- Buyer and seller both carry risk, so both must show commitment.
- Blockchain must be invisible for usability but verifiable for trust.
- Reputation must come from real transaction events, not empty claims.
- The MVP must be honest about what is simulated and what is on-chain.
