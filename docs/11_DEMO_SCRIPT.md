# 11 - Demo Script

Target duration: 3-5 minutes.

## Demo setup

Demo commodity: Red chili / bird's eye chili.

Seller: Probolinggo Chili Supplier.
Buyer: Surabaya Restaurant Group.
Deal value: IDR 20,000,000.

Buyer deposit:

- principal: IDR 20,000,000;
- buyer bond: IDR 1,000,000;
- buyer fee: IDR 100,000;
- total: IDR 21,100,000.

Seller deposit:

- seller bond: IDR 1,000,000;
- seller fee: IDR 100,000;
- total: IDR 1,100,000.

## Demo flow

1. Open landing page.
2. Explain the problem: discovery alone does not solve payment trust, evidence continuity, or reputational memory.
3. Explain the corridor: discovery -> recorded negotiation -> mutual Open Deal Room -> funding -> lock -> proof -> settlement -> reputation.
4. Open marketplace.
5. Click the chili listing.
6. Open the seller profile and explain verified volume, role-specific reputation, and proof visibility.
7. Click `Submit Offer`.
8. Show that the user lands directly in the recorded negotiation workspace rather than a detached staging form.
9. Explain that negotiation is recorded before escrow begins and that the first offer includes editable commercial terms inside the Deal Terms panel.
10. Switch roles so the counterparty clicks `Accept Offer` inside the Deal Terms panel.
11. Show that `Open Deal Room` only becomes available after the terms are accepted.
12. Switch roles so both buyer and seller click `Open Deal Room`, then enter the active Deal Room.
13. Show the funding obligations, deposit window, and the local bank versus crypto wallet rail story.
14. Switch role to Buyer and simulate buyer deposit.
15. Switch role to Seller and simulate seller deposit.
16. Show escrow locked and the Stellar trust panel.
17. Explain why blockchain stays mostly invisible until trust proof is needed.
18. Submit proof/evidence.
19. Show proof hash.
20. Accept delivery.
21. Show the settled room, including the settlement record and final wallet-routing summary.
22. Return to the profile or room summary and show the updated reputation ledger for both parties.
23. Finish with tagline.

## Final pitch line

Settleway helps real commodity buyers and sellers move from discovery to settlement with negotiation, escrow, proof, and reputation that can actually be verified.
