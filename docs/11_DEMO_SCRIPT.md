# 11 - Demo Script

Target duration: 3-5 minutes.

## Demo setup

Demo commodity: Red chili / cabai rawit merah.

Seller: Probolinggo Chili Supplier.
Buyer: Surabaya Restaurant Group.
Deal value: IDR 20,000,000.

Buyer deposit:

- principal: IDR 20,000,000;
- buyer bond: IDR 1,000,000;
- buyer fee: IDR 100,000;
- total: IDR 21,100,000.

Seller deposit:

- seller bond: IDR 1,000,000;
- seller fee: IDR 100,000;
- total: IDR 1,100,000.

## Demo flow

1. Open landing page.
2. Explain the problem: marketplace discovery is not enough for high-value trade.
3. Open marketplace.
4. Click chili listing.
5. Show seller reputation.
6. Open Deal Room.
7. Show money breakdown.
8. Switch role to Buyer and simulate buyer deposit.
9. Switch role to Seller and simulate seller deposit.
10. Show escrow locked.
11. Submit proof/evidence.
12. Show proof hash.
13. Accept delivery.
14. Show completed settlement.
15. Show Stellar proof panel.
16. Show updated reputation.
17. Finish with tagline.

## Final pitch line

Settleway helps real commodity sellers and buyers move from discovery to settlement with escrow, proof, and reputation that can be verified.
